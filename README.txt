Menu Menace v5
===============

How to play
-----------
Menu Menace is a tiny build-your-own restaurant game. You create your own flavor profiles, ingredients, dishes, and menu categories. Customers order from your menu, you tap ingredients into the cooking pot, then serve the dish for coins and XP.

Starter restaurant
------------------
The starter save has only one example dish:
- 🍝 spaghetti in Classics
- 🍝 pasta in Staples
- 🍅 tomato sauce in Sauces
- 🌿 herbs in Seasonings
- savory/umami, sweet/sweetness, and fresh/freshness

You can delete or edit all of those. They are examples, not required defaults.

Categories
----------
A good name for base foods like pasta, bread, rice, tortillas, burger patties, or a slice of beef is "Staples" or "Bases." In this build, pasta starts in Staples. Sauces and Seasonings are separate examples.

Orders
------
Normal "with" and "with no" requests only use ingredients that belong to that dish's recipe. So spaghetti will not randomly ask for no cheese unless cheese is actually part of the spaghetti recipe.

Rare chaotic orders can ask for a strange add-on from outside the recipe, but the customer will say it is weird on purpose.

Editing
-------
Use the pencil/edit buttons in Build to open detail popovers for:
- flavor profiles
- ingredients, including icon, category, and flavor amounts
- dishes, including name, icon, category, wording, price, base recipe, and optional ingredients

Cosmetics
---------
Cosmetics are in their own tab. Spend gems to unlock themes. You get gems when you level up.

Saving
------
The game autosaves in your browser. Export Save downloads a file named with your restaurant and chef name, like:
Little Pizzeria - Maria.menu-menace-save.json

To publish on GitHub Pages
--------------------------
Upload index.html, style.css, script.js, and README.txt together in the same folder. If changes do not show up right away, refresh the page or close and reopen the browser tab because Safari and GitHub Pages can cache older files.
